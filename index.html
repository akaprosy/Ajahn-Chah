Ajahn Chah

Influential teacher of the Buddhadhamm

Phra Bodhiñāṇathera(Chah Subaddho)


Timeline of Ajahn Chah's life:


  • 1918- Ajahn Chah was born
  • 1927- Entered monestry as novice
  • 1939- Was ordained as a Theravadan monk (or bhikkhu).
  • 1946- Left monestry to practice as an Ascetic Monk
  • 1954- Wat Nong Pah Pong established where Ajah Chah would teach meditation practice
  • 1979- Saw the establishment of Chithurst Buddhist Monastery, the first branch of Wat Nong Pah Pong outside Thailand
  • 1992- Passed away at age 73
